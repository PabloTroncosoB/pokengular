About Pokengular

Pokengular is a tiny experiment I built. You smash two pokemon names together, hit Go!, and an AI draws whatever comes out. Every roll picks a random quality (hideous, atrocious, happy, cute, sickly, derpy, powerful or muscular) and a fresh seed — so no two generations are the same.

How it works

Type something like vulpchu and hit Go! (or just Enter). The app finds where to split — vulp + chu = vulpix + pikachu — and ships the rest off to Pollinations. Hit Regenerate to roll again.

What's in here

  • Angular (standalone components, signals, reactive forms)
  • Pollinations.ai for the image generation
  • BYOP — bring your own pollen
